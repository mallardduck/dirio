S3 POST Policy Upload

Fill in the fields below with the values produced by mc share upload or boto3.generate_presigned_post(), then choose a file and click Upload.

How it works

POST /mybucket  HTTP/1.1
Content-Type: multipart/form-data; boundary=...

--boundary
Content-Disposition: form-data; name="key"

uploads/myfile.txt
--boundary
Content-Disposition: form-data; name="x-amz-algorithm"

AWS4-HMAC-SHA256
--boundary
Content-Disposition: form-data; name="x-amz-credential"

AKIAIOSFODNN7EXAMPLE/20240101/us-east-1/s3/aws4_request
--boundary
Content-Disposition: form-data; name="x-amz-date"

20240101T120000Z
--boundary
Content-Disposition: form-data; name="x-amz-signature"

<hex-encoded HMAC-SHA256 over base64 policy>
--boundary
Content-Disposition: form-data; name="policy"

<base64-encoded JSON policy document>
--boundary
Content-Disposition: form-data; name="file"; filename="myfile.txt"
Content-Type: application/octet-stream

<file bytes>
--boundary--

Success responses

  • Default (no success field): 204 No Content
  • success_action_status=200: 200 OK (empty body)
  • success_action_status=201: 201 Created with PostResponse XML
  • success_action_redirect=https://…: 303 See Other with ?bucket=&key=&etag=
